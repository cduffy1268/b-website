Game 2

This game is pretty simple. All you have to do is shoot the circles that are flying towards you. Your character will move to wherever your mouse is moving towards. To destroy the circles you will use left click to shoot your projectiles. You score 10 for each hit and 100 for each destroyed. Good luck and have fun!!
